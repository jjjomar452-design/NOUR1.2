MY QUEEN

To the one who turned my world into a beautiful masterpiece.
They say that love is just a word until someone comes along and gives it meaning. With you, I didn't just find love; I found my best friend, my peace,
and my home. Every moment we spend together is a memory I want to keep forever,
and every smile you give me is a reason to love this life even more. You are my forever and always.
